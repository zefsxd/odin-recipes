Bolognese

Desciption

This recipe outlines how to cook an 8hr bologenese sauce

Taking 8hrs it is best to take the day off work to make this

Ingredients

  • 1tbsp vegetable oil
  • 3tbsp butter plus 1 tbsp for tossing pasta
  • 1/2cup chopped onion
  • 2/3cup chopped celery
  • 2/3cup chopped carrot
  • 3/4lb ground beef
  • salt
  • black pepper
  • 1cup whole milk
  • nutmeg
  • 1cup dry white wine
  • 1 1/2 cup plum tomatoes w juice
  • 1lb pasta

Steps

  1. Put oil, butter and onion in pot and turn heat to medium. Cook and stir the onion until translucent, then add chopped celery and carrot. Cookf or 2 minutes, stirring to coat vegetables well
  2. Add ground beef, salt, pepper. Crumble meat with fork and stir well.
  3. Add milk and simmer until gone, add nutmeg and stir
  4. Add wine, then tomatoes and stir. When tomatoes bubble turn down to lazy simmer, cook for 3hrs.
  5. Toss with drained pasta, add tbsp of butter
