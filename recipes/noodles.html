Noodles

Description

These noodles will leave you feeling as if you ate a bowl full of worms

Add tomato sauce and cheese for extra flavour

Ingredients

  • Packet of 2 minute noodles
  • Boiling water

Steps

  1. Open packet of noodles
  2. Place in bowl with sachet
  3. Boil water
  4. Cover noodles with boiling water
  5. Cook on high in microwave for 2min
  6. Drain, enjoy
